My favorite destinations until now

One of my favorite places in Europe is London, England. Whether you're looking for art or architecture, food or football, London has been honed into a genuinely incredible city through its 2,000-year history.
London is famous for its art, architecture, food and fashion, some of which it does better than anywhere else in the world.

The best museums in London are (all are free!!!)

Name About
British Museum Step into the world-famous British Museum and revel in its amazing collection of rare and historic artefacts from around the world. Highlights include the Rosetta Stone, Parthenon sculptures and Egyptian mummies.
Victoria and Albert Museum (V&A) Be dazzled by the eclectic art and design on offer at the Victoria and Albert Museum. Its vast collections include everything from ceramics and fashion to wallpapers and jewellery, spanning 5,000 years.
Imperial War Museum London Discover the realities of modern wartime history at IWM London, from the First World War to the present day. Its permanent displays include Curiosities of War, The Holocaust Exhibition and the First World War Galleries.
Natural History Museum Explore our planet's living wonders at the Natural History Museum. Inside the striking building, favourites include the Dinosaurs gallery, the blue whale model and the state-of-the-art Darwin Centre, where you can see hundreds of specimens as well as scientists at work.
Science Museum Get hands-on at the Science Museum's interactive galleries and simulators. Trace the journey of scientific breakthroughs, then visit the in-house IMAX 3D cinema and take a trip deep underwater or zoom high up into space.
